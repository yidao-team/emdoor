Rugged Mobile

RComputer Provider

Yidao Information (stock code 001314.SZ), founded in 2008, has several subsidiaries including Yidao Digital, Yijing Virtual, and Yidao Research Institute. Its business covers consumer mobile terminals, industry application terminals, AI smart hardware, VR/AR/MR and many other fields, and is committed to making cutting-edge technology more accessible!

In the future, Emdoor will adhere to the development of innovation, cooperation, openness and win-win, stay close to customers and partners, work together to build and walk together, and help promote the industry and enhance the value of the industry through the power of ruggedized mobile computers.

Strong R&D team

We have a strong R&D team, with R&D personnel accounting for more than 40% of the company. Team members are actively engaged in innovation and have more than 600 intellectual property rights such as patents and software works.

With the team"s professional capabilities and keen insight into customer needs, we have successfully designed and developed more than 200 ruggedized mobile computer products in recent years to continuously meet the diverse needs of customers.

Why choose Emdoor

Since 2012, Emdoor has been involved in the customization and R&D of rugged computer solutions in the industry, and insists on designing and developing rugged products for harsh environments.

We have a mature business model and adopt a comprehensive manufacturing system from PCBA production to product assembly.

Through more than 10 years of accumulation, we have undoubtedly become one of the best rugged computer solution companies in China and one of the largest rugged PC manufacturer companies in China.
